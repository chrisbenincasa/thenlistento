Welcome to ThenListenTobeta

Overview

Finding new music can be a drag. ThenListenTo is here to help.

Enter an artist or song name, genre, or mood into the box and hit enter and ThenListenTo will generate a pretty graph of results. The bigger the circle, the better the match!

Click and hold a circle for a second to expand the graph with even more results; it's that simple!

Even share searches with your friends! ThenListenTo generates sharable links that direct a user right back to their search!

But there's more! Check out the help section for a full list of commands!

Technical

So how does ThenListenTo work?

ThenListenTo takes advantage of a some great HTML5 features and Javascript libraries to produce the graphs you see.

The HTML5 Canvas element feature is used for dynamic graph drawing. The graph drawing itself is achieved by using the Javascript library arbor.js along with jQuery. The system makes asynchronous calls to the Last.fm and EchoNest databases to produce accurate results for artist, track, and genre recommendations.

ThenListenTo uses carefully designed regular expressions to recognize English syntax. These expressions are constantly being stress-tested to ensure that the user is delivered the search experience they expect.
