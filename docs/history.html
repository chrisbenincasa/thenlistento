Influence Searches

Then Listen To can help you learn about your favorite band's roots. Searching for a band's influences is a unique way of discovering new music. In order to perform an influence search, clicked the "History" link on the home page.

In the new form, searches are formatted like so: "influenced {artist name}". Then Listen To will generate a cool graph that shows artists that influenced the artist that was entered.

Click an example to try it out!

  • Who Influenced John Coltrane?
  • Who Influenced Nirvana?
  • Who Influenced Radiohead?

Who Influenced Searches

If we can look back to see who influenced an artist, why not look at more recent times to see who an artist influenced? This is possible with Who Influenced Searches in Then Listen To.

Who Influenced Searches generally take the form: "did {artist name} influence". See the reverse of the examples above by clicking on an example below.

Click an example to try it out!

  • Who did John Coltrane influence?
  • Who did Nirvana influence?
  • Who did Radiohead influence?
