Then Listen To makes it easy to expand your graphs. If you see an artist in a graph that you want recommendations for, simply click that artist's circle for a second. A new search is appended to your existing graph with 10 new recommendations!

Hovering over an artist's node will reveal all of its direct links and temporarily fade out all unrelated nodes. This is useful when viewing complex graphs with many nodes (generally after 4 or more searches on the same graph).
