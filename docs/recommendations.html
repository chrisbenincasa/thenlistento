Artist Searches

Artist search is at the center of ThenListenTo. This means that a simple search without any additional parameters will be interpreted as an artist search before any other options are explored by the system

To search for an artist, simply type the artist's and hit enter.

Click an example to try it out!

  • Radiohead
  • Led Zeppelin
  • Miles Davis

Track Searches

You can even get track recommendations! Search for a track by entering "{track name} by {artist name}", without quotes. ThenListenTo will know you want a track and not an artist or genre. You can also flag your search with "track: " or "song: " if you don't remember the artist's name!

Click an example to try it out!

  • 15 Step by Radiohead
  • track: Stairway to Heaven
  • song: I Want To Hold Your Hand

ThenListenTo will always assume a search is an artist name first. To force a track name search, flag it or utilize the advanced search options.

Genre Searches

Searching artists and tracks is specific. If you wish to get generalized recommendations on a genre, mood, or tag, you're in luck! ThenListenTo provides means to recommend artists based on these characteristics. In order to do so, first flag your search with "genre: ", "mood: ", or "tag: ".

Click an example to try it out!

  • genre: rock
  • mood: happy
  • tag: loud

Don't forget the colon after your chosen flag!
